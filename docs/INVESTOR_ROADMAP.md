# Nettreemarket – Investor Roadmap

## Phase 1 – Foundation (Completed)
- Core platform architecture defined
- Repository and documentation structure established
- Initial compliance-first and modular design
- Market problem and positioning clearly articulated

## Phase 2 – MVP Validation (Next 3–6 Months)
- Launch controlled MVP with selected B2B participants
- Validate demand, pricing logic, and transaction flow
- Integrate basic quality verification and data capture
- Establish first strategic trade and logistics partners

## Phase 3 – Scale & Automation (6–18 Months)
- Expand to additional regional trade hubs
- Introduce AI-assisted grading, pricing, and inventory logic
- Strengthen compliance, traceability, and audit layers
- Prepare platform for higher transaction volumes

## Phase 4 – Platform Expansion (18+ Months)
- Enable advanced financial, ESG, and asset-tokenization modules
- Position Nettreemarket as a global digital infrastructure layer
- Strategic partnerships with institutional players
- Long-term monetization and ecosystem growth

## Capital Utilization Focus
- Product development and platform stability
- Strategic partnerships and onboarding
- Compliance, legal, and infrastructure readiness
- Preparing the foundation for scalable growth
